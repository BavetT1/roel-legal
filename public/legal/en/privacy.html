Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2, 2026

ROEL ("we", "our", "the app") is a camera application for iOS. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

  • All photos are processed locally on your device using the GPU.
  • Photos are saved to your device's Photo Library and never uploaded to our servers.
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

2. Camera & Photo Library Access

The app requests access to your camera and photo library solely to take and save photos. These permissions can be revoked at any time in iOS Settings.

3. Subscriptions

ROEL Pro subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app user ID — no email, name, or other personal information is shared. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

4. Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat — subscription management (anonymous ID only)
  • Apple App Store — payment processing

5. Data Retention

We do not store any user data on external servers. All app settings are stored locally on your device via UserDefaults and are deleted when the app is uninstalled.

6. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
